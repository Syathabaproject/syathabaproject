About Syathaba Boxing Promotions

At Syathaba Boxing Promotions, we believe in the power of sport to bring about change. We are dedicated to developing boxing talent in South Africa and organizing high-quality events that put our athletes and partners in the spotlight.

Syathaba Career Day - Inchanga, KZN

At Inchanga, Cato Ridge, KZN. A small developing region, with a large diverse population. A remote community facing many development challenges at extremes, with little success in Social development aspirations. Renewed efforts and long term partnerships restore hope in successful development aspirations moving into the future. Our company invited Matric Learners and academic institution marketing teams as well as government stakeholders to inchanga for university applications and workshop ed learners on the various possibilities in the career space.
Our director Mr. BS Vilakazi is from Inchanga and has an empirical bond to this area.
